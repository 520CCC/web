About 2048

2048 is a popular number puzzle game where the goal is to slide numbered tiles on a grid to merge them and create a tile with the number 2048.

The game was created by Italian developer Gabriele Cirulli and was first released in March 2014.

The rules of the game are simple to understand but difficult to master. Players can continuously move tiles and merge them to achieve higher scores.

2048 has gained widespread popularity worldwide and has become a favorite pastime for many.
